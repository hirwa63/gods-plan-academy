A Legacy of Education and Faith

God's Plan Academy Musanze is more than just a school; it's a vibrant community dedicated to holistic development.

Founded with a vision to combine academic rigour with Christian values, we provide a nurturing environment where children from Nursery through Primary school discover their potential. Our state-of-the-art facility in Musanze offers small class sizes, personalized attention, and a competency-based curriculum that prepares students for the challenges of a global society. We emphasize critical thinking, creativity, and spiritual growth, ensuring our graduates are not only knowledgeable but also compassionate, resilient, and principled leaders.
