Our Story

Building a foundation of faith and excellence since 2005.

A Vision for Excellence

Founded with a vision to combine academic rigour with Christian values, God's Plan Academy has grown from a small community initiative into one of Musanze's most respected primary schools.

Our mission is to provide a holistic education that nurtures the mind, body, and spirit of every child, preparing them to be the leaders of tomorrow in Rwanda and beyond.
