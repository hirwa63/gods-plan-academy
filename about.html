Our Story

Founded With a Higher Purpose

God's Plan Academy was founded in 2005 in Musanze, Rwanda with a simple conviction: every child is a gift from God, deserving quality education, unconditional love and the opportunity to fulfil their unique potential.

What started as a small nursery with 30 pupils has grown into a thriving community of over 450 pupils, 35 teachers and countless graduates making a difference across Rwanda and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic, faith-based education that nurtures the intellectual, spiritual, social and physical development of every child — equipping them to serve God, Rwanda and the world.
